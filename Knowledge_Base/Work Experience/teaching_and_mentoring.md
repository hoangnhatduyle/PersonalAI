## Teaching Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Meet students where they are:** Understand their current knowledge level and build from there
2. **Create safe learning environment:** Encourage questions without judgment
3. **Explain the "why," not just the "how":** Help students understand underlying principles
4. **Learn by doing:** Encourage hands-on practice and experimentation
5. **Celebrate progress:** Recognize improvement and build confidence

### Application to Professional Settings
These teaching principles apply directly to:
- **Onboarding new team members:** Helping them ramp up quickly and confidently
- **Code reviews:** Providing constructive feedback that educates, not just criticizes
- **Knowledge sharing:** Creating documentation and presentations that are accessible
- **Mentorship:** Supporting junior engineers' growth and career development
