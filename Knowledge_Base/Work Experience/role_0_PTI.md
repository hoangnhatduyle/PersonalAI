## Resume Summary

First professional software engineering co-op. Enhanced PTI's internal customer-facing website and built simulation tooling for the Simulation Department using .NET Framework, C#, and MSSQL. Implemented validated mathematical algorithms for plastic bottle/preform volume calculations and graphing utilities to support engineering analysis. Gained cross-functional exposure by assisting simulation engineers with lab measurements, testing, and customer measurement sessions.

---

## Responsibilities

### Web Development & Maintenance
- Maintained and enhanced the internal customer-facing website using .NET Framework, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and MSSQL to improve daily operations and data access
- Modified and enhanced existing web applications for internal company use; followed development standards

### Simulation Software Development
- Designed and implemented mathematical methods and algorithms to visualize shapes and compute volumes for plastic bottles and preforms used by the Simulation Department
- Developed graphing utilities that supported simulation accuracy and simplified engineers' analysis workflows
- Worked on tools to improve day-to-day operations within the company

### Engineering Support
- Assisted senior engineers with lab measurements, test setups, and documented simulation results in written reports for stakeholders
- Supported occasional customer visits by collecting measurement data and operating injection/blow molding equipment under supervision
- Generated and presented reports on simulation results of products
