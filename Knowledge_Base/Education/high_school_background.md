## School Context

### Gifted School System in Vietnam

**Nguyen Thuong Hien High School For The Gifted** is part of Vietnam's specialized high school system designed for academically talented students. These "gifted schools" (trường chuyên) provide:

- **Accelerated curriculum** with advanced coursework in science and mathematics
- **Competitive admission** through entrance examinations
- **College preparatory focus** with rigorous academic standards
- **Specialization tracks** allowing students to focus on specific subjects

### Natural Sciences Track

The Natural Sciences track emphasizes:
- **Mathematics** (advanced calculus, algebra, geometry)
- **Physics** (mechanics, electricity, thermodynamics)
- **Chemistry** (general, organic, analytical chemistry)
- **Biology** (cellular biology, genetics)
- **Preparation for university** science and engineering programs

This track is designed for students planning to pursue STEM fields in higher education.
