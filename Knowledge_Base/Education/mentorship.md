## Influential mentor

Professor Lawrence Thomas has been one of the most influential figures in my academic journey. Although his teaching style felt demanding at first, I quickly recognized that his rigor came from a deep commitment to student growth. By pushing myself to keep up with his lectures and tackle challenging projects, I developed both technical ability and professional discipline.

### Notable projects under his mentorship

- Huffman Encoding implementation
- AES & DES cryptography implementations
- Software Engineering (Air3550) project

Working on these assignments strengthened my problem-solving skills and taught me how to approach complex, open-ended engineering problems.

### Lessons & lasting impact

From this experience I adopted a guiding philosophy: every mentor has strengths and weaknesses, and there is always something valuable to learn. I became more observant, open-minded, and willing to step outside my comfort zone — because growth often comes from embracing difficult problems.

### Short quote

"Professor Thomas taught me that real growth comes from challenging work and rigorous expectations."
