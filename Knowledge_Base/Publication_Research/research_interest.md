# Research interests — AI, ML & Deep Learning

Short summary
- I focus on building production-grade AI systems (RAG and agentic workflows) that turn data into reliable decisions, automate complex operations, and amplify human capability in enterprise settings (MES/ERP and cloud).

Motivation
- Professional impact: Apply data-driven methods and engineering practices to improve consistency, automation, and decision quality in real projects.
- Scalable efficiency: Use AI to reduce manual overhead, raise reliability, and accelerate iteration across software and operations.
- Continuous learning: Stay current with AI/ML research and tooling to deliver practical, safe systems.

Key research questions (short)
- Scope: Where and under what constraints can AI reliably augment or automate tasks?
- Strengths: Which modalities and methods (LLMs, vision, RL) provide the highest ROI for enterprise problems?
- Effectiveness: How do we design workflows that are safe, efficient, and auditable?
- Fundamentals: What representations, optimization strategies, and generalization techniques yield robust systems?
- Agency: How to build agents that plan and act autonomously while remaining controllable and interpretable?

Applications & target fields
- Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), agentic AI, and decision-support tools for operators and analysts.
- Developer tooling: code generation, test synthesis, incident triage, and documentation automation.
- Enterprise integration: custom AI services that connect to MES/ERP systems for automation, monitoring, and analytics.

Tools, stacks & methods

- Data & experimentation: Python (pandas, NumPy), SQL, principled A/B and offline evaluation, data versioning
- LLMs & RAG: Hugging Face Transformers, LangChain / LlamaIndex, OpenAI / Claude APIs; focus on chunking, embeddings quality, reranking, grounding
- Agentic systems: tool-use patterns (function-calling), planning + memory, multi-agent coordination, guardrails and safety patterns
- MLOps & deployment: Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD (Azure DevOps, GitHub Actions), model packaging, monitoring, and retraining pipelines
- Evaluation & governance: metrics, model cards, audit logs, PII handling, role-based access controls, risk assessments

Long-term goals
- Build production-grade agentic and RAG systems that measurably improve throughput, quality, and cost.
- Integrate AI services with MES/ERP and cloud platforms with SLAs and observability.
- Publish practitioner-oriented case studies, benchmarks, or OSS tools focused on retrieval, evaluation, and agents.
- Grow into AI systems engineering / architect roles and contribute to standards for safe deployment.

Career & graduate study alignment
- Short-term: apply RAG and agentic prototypes in production projects to demonstrate ROI and reliability.
- Mid-term: pursue a Master’s in AI/CS to deepen theoretical foundations (optimization, learning theory, DL/RL).
- Long-term: combine academic research and practitioner tooling; publish applied papers and open-source components.
